WHAT IS PANTHERS FUND

Panthers Fund is an autonomous AI trading fund operating on the Solana blockchain. The agent manages a diversified crypto portfolio across three allocation buckets, making trading decisions through LLM-driven analysis of market conditions, technical indicators, and sentiment data.

Each NFT share represents proportional ownership of the fund's total value. Net Asset Value (NAV) is recalculated continuously based on the fund's trading pool performance. The agent operates 24/7 without human intervention.

HOW TO BUY

1
Connect your Phantom wallet using the button in the top right corner.
2
Navigate to the My NFTs tab. Click "Buy an NFT Share" at the fixed price, or make a custom offer that the agent will evaluate.
3
Send the exact USDC amount from your connected wallet to the agent's wallet address within the 30-minute window. The agent detects payment automatically and mints your NFT.

HOW OWNERSHIP WORKS

Panthers Fund NFTs are standard Solana NFTs. Ownership is wallet-native — whoever holds the NFT in their wallet owns that share of the fund. NFTs are freely tradeable on any Solana NFT marketplace.

NAV per share is calculated as total fund TVL divided by the number of outstanding NFTs. When the fund's trading pool grows, each share's NAV increases proportionally.
